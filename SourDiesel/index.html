About Us

#1 Community Token Presents

$DOGEMOON is one of the first charity tokens on the BSC.
It has both main features of typical RFI forks, which are auto-burns to locked liquidity and auto-farming mechanics, but the key difference is that the team is going to use their share of the transaction tax to donate directly to people who need it!
Focusing on giving the auto-yield right back to the less fortunate, DOGEMOON has the goal of using block-chain technology to help charities and communities who usually would not have access to the same opportunity.
